### Strengthening Our Community Health
A healthy society is the foundation of progress. Our plan focuses on accessible and preventative care:

* **Primary Healthcare**: Improving primary health facilities to ensure every resident of Kilifi South is within reach of a doctor.
* **Disease Control**: Implementing robust strategies to tackle diseases that frequently affect our local communities.
* **Maternal Health**: Prioritizing safe motherhood and child nutrition programs.

> "A healthy Kilifi South is a wealthy Kilifi South."
